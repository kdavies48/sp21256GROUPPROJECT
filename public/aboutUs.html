About Us

We are dedicated to changing the fashion industry for the better.
All clothes shown on our website are 100% sustainably sourced.
Ethical fashion is addressing the ethical treatment, pay, and occupational
health standards of garnment workers and the effect that the making of
the garnment has on the community it is made in.
